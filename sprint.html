Team Rules

How we run this

No AI in Phase I and II.
Rules-based scoring. Tag-based path. Get Compass working first. AI is Phase IV. Canver leads it when the time comes.
Kevin writes questions. David approves.
Not the other way around. Kevin owns survey content and logic. David reviews and signs off. Due Day 7.
Mohammed designs. Canver builds.
Mohammed proposes UI/UX. Canver translates to production code. No role conflict — collaboration is the model.
Advisor Console ships in Phase III, not Phase II.
Excluded from first sprint per team agreement. Compass and dashboard ship first. Advisor Console follows in Phase III.
Deepu leads launch. Canver leads AI.
Deepu owns MN content migration, market strategy, and Phase V launch execution. Canver owns AI integration in Phase IV.
Two-week sprint cycles with clear deliverables.
Each phase = one sprint. Deliverables defined upfront. Pinned in shared channel. No stagnation.
